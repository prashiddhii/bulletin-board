Notices

Agricultural machinery distribution program

Date: 2081-10-08

The Nagarpalika is pleased to announce the Agricultural Machinery Distribution Program to support local farmers by providing access to modern farming equipment such as tractors, plows, seeders, and irrigation pumps. This initiative aims to enhance agricultural productivity and promote mechanized farming. Eligible farmers, cooperatives, and agricultural organizations are encouraged to apply through the Municipal Agriculture Office from 2081-11-01 to 2081-12-4. Priority will be given to small and medium-scale farmers, with training provided for proper equipment use. For inquiries, visit the Municipal Agriculture Office or contact us at 98XXXXXXXX/ xxx@municipality.gov.np
